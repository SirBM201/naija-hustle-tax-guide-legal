Naija Hustle Tax Guide respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data associated with our WhatsApp service.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, use either method below:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to: bms.concept@hotmail.com
Include your WhatsApp phone number and use the subject:
Data Deletion Request – Naija Hustle Tax Guide

Option 2: WhatsApp Request

Send the message DELETE MY DATA to our official WhatsApp business number.
(If you want, replace this line with your exact WhatsApp business number.)

What Happens Next

  • Your request will be verified
  • All associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Some data may be retained if required by law or regulatory obligations.
